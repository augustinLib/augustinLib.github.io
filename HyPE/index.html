Paper Code

HyPE conducts a pseudo-reasoning process by generating the hierarchical category path step-by-step to decode docid, allowing it to serve as an explanation which enhances explainability.

Why HyPE?

Consistently improves retrieval accuracy across diverse docid formats
Generates high-quality explanations preferred by human judges
Helps users identify relevant documents with higher accuracy and confidence

Better Decisions in Realistic Search Scenarios

Like in Google search, users often judge relevance from titles and short snippets alone.
HYPE's category paths provide clarity—enabling accurate selections and confident decisions.
